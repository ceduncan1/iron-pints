The Original Ragin Cage'n began when Nicholas Cage discovered his passion for gourmet cuisin. In addition to his distinguished acting career he began dabbling in the culinary world. Wanting to share his passion for food with others, Cage opened The Ragin Cage'n. At the grand opening friends and family showed up in support. John Travolta was in attendance and commented "Nick's food is the absolute best!" After rave reviews he opened several other locations in New York and Tokyo. Our signature dish, the 12 oz. Prime Japanese Kurobuta Black Hog Chop is a favorite along with the Farmed Ostrich Filet Mignon are favorites of regulars. We passionately serve dishes with only the finest ingredients creating a dining experience as unique as the owner himself.
